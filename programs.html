What We Offer

Our Programmes

We offer a structured residential rehabilitation program that includes:

01

Medical and Psychological Assessment

Comprehensive medical and psychological evaluations to understand each client's unique needs and develop a personalised treatment plan.

02

Individual Counseling and Therapy

One-on-one sessions with certified counsellors using evidence-based approaches including CBT and motivational interviewing.

03

Group Therapy and Support Meetings (AA/NA)

Structured group sessions and 12-step support meetings that build community and shared accountability in recovery.

04

Psycho-education on Addiction and Recovery

Educational sessions that help clients understand the science of addiction, triggers, and the path to lasting recovery.

05

Spiritual Nourishment and Bible Study

Faith-based support through prayer, Bible study, and spiritual counselling to nurture inner healing and purpose.

06

Life Skills and Personal Development

Training in essential life skills including financial literacy, communication, and goal-setting for a successful reintegration into society.

07

Family Counseling and Reconciliation

Guided family sessions that rebuild trust, improve communication, and create a supportive home environment for recovery.

08

Relapse Prevention Training

Practical strategies and coping skills to identify triggers, manage cravings, and maintain long-term sobriety.

09

Recreational and Therapeutic Activities

Sports, art therapy, nature walks, and other activities that promote physical wellness and emotional healing.
