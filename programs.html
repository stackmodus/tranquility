What We Offer

Treatment Programs

Personalised treatment plans designed to address the root causes of addiction and mental health challenges.

🌱

Inpatient Rehabilitation

Our 90-day residential programme provides 24/7 care in a structured, supportive environment with individual and group therapy sessions.

  • Medical detoxification
  • Individual counselling
  • Group therapy
  • Family involvement
👥

Outpatient Programme

Flexible treatment that allows clients to maintain daily responsibilities while receiving structured therapeutic support.

  • Weekly therapy sessions
  • Support groups
  • Relapse prevention
  • Skills training
💖

Mental Health Treatment

Comprehensive care for depression, anxiety, trauma, and co-occurring disorders alongside addiction treatment.

  • Psychiatric evaluation
  • CBT & DBT therapy
  • Trauma-informed care
  • Medication management
🌟

Aftercare & Sober Living

Continued support after primary treatment to help maintain sobriety and build a strong foundation for long-term recovery.

  • Alumni network
  • Mentorship programme
  • Transitional housing
  • Employment support
🌴

Holistic Therapies

Complementary approaches that nurture mind, body, and spirit as part of comprehensive recovery.

  • Yoga & meditation
  • Art & music therapy
  • Fitness & nutrition
  • Nature walks
👪

Family Programme

Healing the entire family unit through education, therapy, and rebuilding trust and communication.

  • Family counselling
  • Education workshops
  • Communication skills
  • Boundary setting
