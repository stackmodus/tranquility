Who We Are

A Sanctuary for Recovery

As the first rehabilitation centre in Trans Nzoia County, Tranquility Rehab Centre is pioneering accessible, world-class recovery services in a region that has long needed them. Nestled in the serene landscapes of western Kenya, we provide a peaceful environment where individuals can break free from addiction and reclaim their lives.

What We Offer

Treatment Programs

Personalised treatment plans designed to address the root causes of addiction and mental health challenges.

🌱

Inpatient Rehabilitation

Our 90-day residential programme provides 24/7 care in a structured, supportive environment with individual and group therapy sessions.

  • Medical detoxification
  • Individual counselling
  • Group therapy
  • Family involvement
👥

Outpatient Programme

Flexible treatment that allows clients to maintain daily responsibilities while receiving structured therapeutic support.

  • Weekly therapy sessions
  • Support groups
  • Relapse prevention
  • Skills training
💖

Mental Health Treatment

Comprehensive care for depression, anxiety, trauma, and co-occurring disorders alongside addiction treatment.

  • Psychiatric evaluation
  • CBT & DBT therapy
  • Trauma-informed care
  • Medication management
🌟

Aftercare & Sober Living

Continued support after primary treatment to help maintain sobriety and build a strong foundation for long-term recovery.

  • Alumni network
  • Mentorship programme
  • Transitional housing
  • Employment support
🌴

Holistic Therapies

Complementary approaches that nurture mind, body, and spirit as part of comprehensive recovery.

  • Yoga & meditation
  • Art & music therapy
  • Fitness & nutrition
  • Nature walks
👪

Family Programme

Healing the entire family unit through education, therapy, and rebuilding trust and communication.

  • Family counselling
  • Education workshops
  • Communication skills
  • Boundary setting
Why Choose Us

What Sets Us Apart

01

First in Trans Nzoia

We are proud to be the first rehabilitation centre in Trans Nzoia County, bringing vital recovery services closer to communities that previously had to travel far for help.

02

Expert Clinical Team

Our multidisciplinary team includes psychiatrists, psychologists, certified addiction counsellors, and social workers with extensive experience.

03

Culturally Sensitive Care

We understand the unique cultural context of addiction in Kenya and East Africa, tailoring our approach to each individual's background.

04

Evidence-Based Methods

We use internationally recognised treatment protocols including CBT, DBT, motivational interviewing, and the 12-step model adapted for our context.

05

Comprehensive Aftercare

Recovery doesn't end at discharge. Our robust aftercare programme provides ongoing support for lasting sobriety and wellness.

06

Affordable & Accessible

We believe quality treatment should be accessible. We offer flexible payment plans and work with insurance providers across Kenya.
