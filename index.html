Who We Are

A Sanctuary for Recovery

As the first rehabilitation centre in Trans Nzoia County, Tranquility Rehab Centre is pioneering accessible, world-class recovery services in a region that has long needed them. Nestled in the serene landscapes of western Kenya, we provide a peaceful environment where individuals can break free from addiction and reclaim their lives.
