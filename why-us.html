Why Choose Us

What Sets Us Apart

We combine world-class treatment with deep understanding of the Kenyan context to deliver recovery that lasts.

01

First in Trans Nzoia

We are proud to be the first rehabilitation centre in Trans Nzoia County, bringing vital recovery services closer to communities that previously had to travel far for help.

02

Expert Clinical Team

Our multidisciplinary team includes psychiatrists, psychologists, certified addiction counsellors, and social workers with extensive experience.

03

Culturally Sensitive Care

We understand the unique cultural context of addiction in Kenya and East Africa, tailoring our approach to each individual's background.

04

Evidence-Based Methods

We use internationally recognised treatment protocols including CBT, DBT, motivational interviewing, and the 12-step model adapted for our context.

05

Comprehensive Aftercare

Recovery doesn't end at discharge. Our robust aftercare programme provides ongoing support for lasting sobriety and wellness.

06

Affordable & Accessible

We believe quality treatment should be accessible. We offer flexible payment plans and work with insurance providers across Kenya.
